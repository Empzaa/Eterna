#City

Cliffrise is a large city nestled in the interior of one of the largest mountains in the region sharing its name. It is unknown if the mountain is a result of human intervention or if it is natural. It has a very unique style of stacked buildings with various tunnel accesses. The buildings, for the most part, are made of terracotta imported from the [[Boba Redar Plains]], dyed various vibrant colors. Due to earthquakes that are common in the region, the houses are designed to flex slightly, using interior wood frames that will bend instead of crack.

The city is home to large amounts of artists, and is commonly referred to as the Painted City due to the sheer amount of art exported from the creative minds of the people.
