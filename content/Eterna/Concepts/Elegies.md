#Concept 

Elegies are the strongest members of their domains, and each [[Domain]] only has one [[Elegy]]. Elegies are immortal in the same way [[Motifs]] are immortal, being unable to die from any conventional means besides massive damage.

The raw domain output of an Elegy outclasses any Motif in their domain, and are naturally far more resilient.

Elegies are able to heal through music like [[Motifs]], but are able to heal far greater injuries alongside being able to heal far quicker.

Elegies lack a heartbeat and are able to survive without food, water, air, and many other essential substances for a regular human. However, the lack of these vital substances is still incredibly uncomfortable for an Elegy.

The strength of Elegies relative to each other vary quite a bit due to the innate combat effectiveness of their respective domains differing quite a bit from Elegy to Elegy. Some Elegies choose to live simple lives, while some choose to take power, and often are able to do so without much difficulty, unless facing many [[Motifs]] or another Elegy.
