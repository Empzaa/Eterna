#City

Cyrus is the happiest city in the world, nestled on the edge of Avalon forest, it a scenic and ideal location. Petals float through the air year round, along with various flower species that provide a cycle of blooming flowers almost every day of the year thanks to the plant engineering done by the governess of Cyrus, Millie Hathow. Millie Hathow is the beloved flora elegy, and is a benevolent force for her city. In the distant past, Millie ousted a tyranical dictator from ruling Cyrus and has ruled well since.
