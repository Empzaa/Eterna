#Person

## **Alias(es) :** Tsutia's Ghost

**Age :** 217 (Elegy since 23, motif from 13-23)
**Occupation :** Gunslinger

**Species :** Human - [[Elegy]]
**[[Domain]] :** Sand

## **Description** 
Evelyn has long brown hair, and regularly goes years without cutting it. She stands at around 5'9 and wears clothes that are about 100 years out of date.

She's an honorable fighter, restricting herself to not use her domain under any circumstance, and purposefully allowing herself to take hits to level out her physical advantage over others who are unlike her. She never uses the healing granted to her by being an Elegy. She's amiable and sarcastic in the right circumstance. She wants to find an equal opponent to die to.

## **Backstory**   
210 years ago, one Mr. Isla got into some bad business with a massive gang in the [[Tsutia Desert]], led by [[Adminius Finch]], the sand [[Elegy]] of the time. Years passed, and Mr. Isla made excuses and excuses about his debts to the gang, and he bought himself time. However, the time that he bought would be the thing that grew his punishment, the hourglass filling with sand. 

His entire family was buried alive, with all dying. A day or so passed, and a revenant pulled herself from the grave of sand. Over the next ten years, using the gun that had been buried alongside her family, she systematically dismantled the gang for her family, without using her newfound abilities once. Eventually, she came face to face with the sand Elegy, and after a long fight, managed to win. Over two hundred bullets were fired from her gun during that fight. She now is a wanderer, going from town to town in the [[Tsutia Desert]] and [[Boba Redar Plains]].
