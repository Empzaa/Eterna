#Concept

After an individual undergoes [[Reprisal]], they become a [[Motif]]. Motifs are individuals who are able to manipulate anything that falls under their [[Domain]]. An example of this would be someone with the fire [[Domain]], who would be able to manipulate fire, and at a stronger level would be able to generate fire as well.

The strength of a Motif can be measured by their [[Tempo]].

After [[Reprisal]], Motifs retain their [[Representative Injury]], and it visibly worsens as their [[Tempo]] slows.
