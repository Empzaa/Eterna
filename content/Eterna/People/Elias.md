#Person

## **Alias(es) :** The Lone Wanderer

**Age :** 570 (Elegy since 17)

**Occupation :** Wanderer


**Species :** Human - [[Elegy]]

**[[Domain]] :** Ice

## **Description** 
Elias has long hair black hair, with a distinct scar across his face, being his representative injury. He stands at about 6'3. He wears practical clothing, simple leather for protection. In battle he wears a tattered red cape that might have once been a flag.

A man with little to keep him in any place for long, and a past of conquest.

Among [[Elegies]], he is considered the strongest.

## **Backstory**
Elias was born in a cold and inhospitable land, and he learned to make it home. When he turned 17, his small settlement faced a man who brought nothing but horrors. Elias was struck down by that man, ice tearing him to bits. In the end, the that man was struck down, and Elias took his place. He was too late to save anything that mattered, and still is.
