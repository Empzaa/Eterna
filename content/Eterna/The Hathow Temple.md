#Location 

Previously the home of the [[Elegy]] proceeding [[Millie Hathow]], it is now her home. The temple is a complex composed of stone and hundreds, if not thousands, of plant species. The entire place operates as a closed in ecosystem that in theory could support hundreds of people. The temple is several miles into [[Avalon Forest]] and its exact location is unknown. The gracious governess usually makes a visit to town once a month or so, ensuring plant growth and stability in the town.
