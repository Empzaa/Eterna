#Region

To put it simply, the Tsutia Desert is unforgiving and inhospitable. 

##### Geography

The Tsutia Desert is a seemingly endless stretch of dune after dune of sand, as if it was designed to be difficult to navigate. Adding to this difficulty of navigation, due to its proximity to the [[Boba Redar Plains]], compasses are unable to function, forcing travelers to navigate by stars. 
##### Climate

The desert is supernaturally hot, forcing travelers to wear specially designed clothes to stand a chance in the heat. These clothes are designed to block direct contact with the sun while retaining as little heat as possible, and are composed of thin layers of breathable fabric that allow for airflow, but block heat.

The biome has two distinct ecosystems, those within the sandstorms, and those exterior to them. The ground beneath the sandstorms gets to below freezing temperatures, which is in stark contrast to the rest of the desert.

These large electric sandstorms are a result of [[Stratusmoths]] in heards.
