#City

Tsutia's very existence is a testament to engineering and architecture. It lies within the inhospitable [[Tsutia Desert]] and lacks any major above ground waterways. The city is a monolith of interconnected buildings with channels of water running all around. The city is functionally a massive stronghold, with water running through its walls to keep residents cool. Water is pumped by a small rotating group of motifs, primarily those with water domains, keeping the city's supply of water fresh and cold. Various contingencies are in place in case of the loss of these motifs. The city is run by an elected jury and is isolationist as a result of its geographic location, taking care of its residents to the best of its abilities.
